Introducing SteemWhales.com - A rankings website for Steemians

Jul 20, 2016 | Hits

Do you want to know who are the leaders of the steem community?

Do you want to know how you rank?

YOU NOW CAN ON STEEM WHALES

Features:

  • Sort by post count, posting rewards, curation rewards, any type of balance, or of course by estimated value (default sort).
  • Search by steem name: useful for small fishes like me.
  • Automatically updating the STEEM price and vesting information. Required to calculate STEEM Power and estimated value.
  • Displays well on most devices. Landscape mode recommended on small phones maybe.

If your account is not yet added on the website, search for the name and it should get added relatively quickly. Also, the website automatically add upvoters from trending topics. You can see how many steemians are currently tracked at the bottom of the website.

I am open to any suggestion, feel free to comment.

Special thanks to @svk for his tool steem-rpc-library-for-javascript-steem-rpc
